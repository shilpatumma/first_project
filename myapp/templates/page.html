THE 2022
ONE PAGE POETRY

Anthology

One Page Poetry is dedicated to the art of expressing a poetic theme on a
single page, whether that poem is four lines or forty, whether it is a love
poem, inspirational poem, philosophical poem, or just one that is fun or
whimsical. The poets represented in this compilation come from all over
the world. To say they are wonderfully talented would be an understate-
ment. They are also remarkably insightful. They are fearless in exploring
themes close to the heart, but also themes that are relevant to our world.
They explore emotions common to so many of us: joy and heartbreak; fear
and fearlessness; hope and love; tolerance and intolerance.

At One Page Poetry, we believe in the bond between head, heart, and soul,
and the poems included here express that bond in ways completely unique
to each poet. Seeing our thoughts and emotions, our philosophies and be-
liefs, our victories and failures come to live on the page is the driving force
behind the One Page journey, and we are honored by individual gifts repre-
sented by the poems within these pages and beyond grateful to the poets
willing to share their poems without reservations and a willingness to be both
vulnerable and courageous.

All proceeds from the sale of this book will go to the Wildlife Fund and
Oceana, two organizations dedicated to the protection of endangered spe-
cies and the preservation of their natural habitats. Thank you for joining us.
